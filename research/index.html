Research研究

AI for structured decision making面向结构化决策的人工智能

My current research taste is shaped by one question: how can intelligent systems help people make better operational decisions when data, uncertainty, and constraints interact? 我当前的研究兴趣可以概括为一个问题：当数据、不确定性与约束同时存在时，智能系统如何帮助人做出更好的运营决策？

01

Learning-augmented optimization学习增强的优化方法

I am interested in how machine learning can support optimization models, including prediction-aware decisions, heuristic design, and data-driven model selection.我关注机器学习如何支持优化建模，包括预测驱动的决策、启发式方法设计，以及基于数据的模型选择。

  • Prediction and optimization under uncertainty不确定性下的预测与优化
  • Learning-based heuristics and search学习型启发式算法与搜索
  • Decision quality evaluation决策质量评估
02

Supply chain and operations systems供应链与运营系统

I study operational problems where demand, capacity, and timing create difficult tradeoffs, especially inventory, scheduling, and resource allocation.我关注需求、产能与时序共同作用下的运营决策问题，尤其是库存、调度和资源配置。

  • Inventory and replenishment decisions库存与补货决策
  • Scheduling and allocation调度与分配
  • Robust and adaptive operations鲁棒与自适应运营
03

LLMs as research and decision interfaces作为研究与决策界面的 LLM

Large language models are useful not only as text generators, but also as interfaces for modeling, retrieval, explanation, and human-in-the-loop decision workflows.大语言模型不仅可以生成文本，也可以成为建模、检索、解释和人机协同决策流程中的交互界面。

  • Paper reading and knowledge management论文阅读与知识管理
  • Natural-language interfaces for optimization优化问题的自然语言界面
  • Reasoning, verification, and workflow design推理、校验与工作流设计
