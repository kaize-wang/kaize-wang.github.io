Why I am building a personal academic website为什么我要搭建个人学术主页

A personal website is not only a profile. It is a place to keep research direction, writing, and project evidence in one durable location.个人网站不只是简介页。它更像一个长期容器，用来保存研究方向、写作和项目证据。

I want this site to be useful before it becomes complete. The first version should make my interests clear, show what I am building, and give collaborators a direct way to understand my current direction.我希望这个网站在“完整”之前就已经有用。第一版要先讲清楚我的兴趣、我正在搭建什么，以及别人如何快速理解我当前的方向。

Blog posts will hold research notes, reading summaries, tool-building reflections, and questions that are still evolving. Compared with social media, a website gives these thoughts a stable address.博客会用来放研究笔记、文献阅读、工具搭建反思，以及还在变化中的问题。和社交媒体相比，网站能给这些想法一个稳定的位置。

The long-term goal is simple: make the site a clear public index of what I care about, what I have done, and what I want to do next.长期目标很简单：让这个网站成为一个清晰的公开索引，说明我关心什么、做过什么、接下来想做什么。
