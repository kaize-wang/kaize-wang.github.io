Paper depth: from phenomena to mechanisms 论文深度：从现象走向机制

A note on why a paper may have data, theory, and literature, yet still feel shallow. 一篇关于“为什么论文有材料、有理论、有文献，却仍然显得不够深入”的思考。

Depth is not knowing more, but explaining better 深度不是知道得更多，而是解释得更细

When a paper is described as “having content but lacking depth,” the first reaction is often confusion. The data are there, the theory is there, and the literature review is not empty. Why does it still fail to satisfy the reader? 当一篇论文被评价为“内容有了，但深度不够”时，最直接的反应往往是困惑：数据有了，理论有了，文献也并不少，为什么还是达不到要求？

I used to think depth came from complicated theories, advanced concepts, or grand narratives. But I now feel that depth is more about the level at which we explain a problem. Many papers stop at “what happened”: listing phenomena, describing facts, and reporting changes. These are necessary, but depth begins when we continue to ask “why”. 我以前容易把深度理解为复杂理论、高级概念或宏大叙事。但现在更倾向于认为，深度取决于我们在什么层次上解释问题。很多论文停留在“发生了什么”：罗列现象、描述事实、报告变化。这些当然必要，但深度真正开始的地方，是继续追问“为什么”。

The same phenomenon can be explained at different levels. A shallow explanation may say that a technology improves efficiency. A deeper one asks how it changes organizational processes. A still deeper one asks why process changes reshape relations among actors, and why similar technologies produce different outcomes in different contexts. 同一个现象可以有不同层次的解释。浅层解释可能说某项技术提高了效率；更进一步，会分析技术如何改变组织流程；再往下，则会追问流程变化为什么改变了主体关系，以及为什么相似技术在不同场景中产生不同结果。

The mechanism behind the phenomenon matters most 真正值得研究的是现象背后的机制

A phenomenon is often visible to many people. A mechanism is not. This is why merely describing a change is usually not enough. If a paper only says that a policy worked, a teaching method changed, or a platform improved efficiency, the reader may still not understand the causal logic behind it. 现象往往是很多人都能看见的，机制却不一定。也正因为如此，单纯描述变化通常不够。如果一篇文章只是说某项政策取得成效、某种教育方式发生变化，或者某个平台提高效率，读者仍然可能不知道背后的因果逻辑是什么。

For research, the phenomenon should be the starting point rather than the endpoint. The more useful questions are: What pushed the change? Which factors interacted? Which assumptions made the mechanism work? Under what conditions would the mechanism fail? 对于研究来说，现象不应该是终点，而应该是起点。更有价值的问题是：是什么推动了这个变化？哪些因素相互作用？这个机制成立依赖哪些假设？在什么条件下它又可能失效？

Depth comes from sustained questioning 深度来自持续追问，而不是快速回答

A paper often feels flat when it rushes to a conclusion too early. Once a phenomenon appears, it immediately gives a single answer. But many research questions need several layers of questioning. If others stop at the first layer and I continue to dig three layers deeper, the paper naturally becomes different. 很多论文写得平，往往是太急于下结论。看到一个现象，就匆忙给出一个答案。但很多研究问题需要一层一层继续追问。别人停在第一层时，如果我能继续往下挖三层，论文自然会不一样。

This also means that “depth awareness” needs daily training. When reading news, essays, or papers, I should not only ask what happened, but also ask what structure produced it, what mechanism maintained it, and what conditions may change it. Good writing habits begin before writing itself. 这也意味着，“深度意识”需要在日常中训练。无论是读新闻、评论还是论文，都不能只问发生了什么，还要继续问是什么结构生成了它、什么机制维持了它、什么条件可能改变它。好的写作习惯，其实在真正写作之前就已经开始了。

Depth is an ability to explain the world 论文深度本质上是一种解释世界的能力

Depth is not making a simple problem artificially complicated. It is not the deliberate piling up of concepts either. The real test is explanatory power: when others see only the result, can I explain the process behind it? When others accept a phenomenon, can I ask about the logic behind it? When others stop at description, can I move toward mechanism? 深度不是把简单问题故意复杂化，也不是刻意堆砌概念。真正考验的是解释能力：当别人只看到结果时，我能不能讲清楚它的形成过程？当别人接受现象时，我能不能追问背后的逻辑？当别人停留在描述时，我能不能继续走向机制？

In this sense, the difference between a flat paper and a deep paper often lies in whether the analysis has reached behind the phenomenon. 从这个意义上说，一篇论文有没有层次，往往就取决于它有没有真正走到现象背后。
